# Day 6 — Exponentials, Logs & Inverses

**Goal:** Achieve complete fluency with exponential and logarithmic behavior so equations, transformations, and inverses feel mechanical and predictable.

**Scope:** Covers **all exponential/log material**  
**Time Expectation:** 6–8 focused hours  
**Rule:** Always think in *inverse pairs* (exponential ↔ logarithmic).
